ROBO-TRACK
MANIA

Tasks

  • Round 1 - Just follow the path by crossing the hurdles and reach to the end point.
  • Round 2 - Two robots will run on the same path and one should reach the end point .(Robots will push each other to cross).

Rules and Regulation

  • There should be maximum 4 members in team.
  • The arena shall be a custom made Racing track with different hurdles.
  • The maximum size of a Robot is 30 * 30 cm, the maximum weight is 2 kg.
  • No external tools should be allowed on the robot which will damage another Robot or Arena.
  • Winner should be decided on the basis of time of completing the task.
  • There are different checkpoints in the racing track.
  • A robot that wanders off the arena surface will be placed on the previous checkpoint.
  • For skip any checkpoint in the track some time period will be deducted.
  • A.C. power supply of 220 v will be provided at the ground and participants will use maximum 24 v of DC input(power adapter).
  • REGISTRATION:400-/per team
