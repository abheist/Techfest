Rules and Regulation

  • Only individual participation is allowed.
  • There will be two Rounds:
    1. Preliminary Round-Online test consisting of questions reguarding C/C++ and Java.
    2. Mains Round-Paricipants will have to write certain programs for the given problems.
  • Questions will differ in marking and difficulty level, there is no negative marking.
  • Criteria for qualifying Preliminary Round will be decided keeping all over performance and all other aspects under consideration (participants will be selected on the cut-off basis).
  • Scores for both the Rounds will be considered for final evaluation .
  • Certificates are provided to the winners of Mains Round only.
