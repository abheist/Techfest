CIRCUIT
DESIGNING

Rules and Regulation

  • Teams of two participants only can contest for this event.
  • There will be total 2 rounds.
  • First round will be elimination round with objective type questions from fields of Electronics.
  • Second round will be circuit designing round in which you will be given circuit diagram with all required components and some extra components. You have to select necessary components and make the circuit.
  • You have to make a circuit in least time possible.
  • After completing the circuit, your circuit will be tested.
  • Team whose circuit works correctly first will be the winner.
  • All other rules will be told at that the time of event.
